TSN-S v1.2.3 Open-Hours Popularity Trend

This update adds an open-hours activity trend panel to the CEO dashboard.

What changed:
- New Open-hours trend card in the dashboard.
- New 7 / 14 / 30 day trend buttons.
- Backend endpoint: /api/activity-trend?days=7
- Trend only counts snapshots captured during TSN-S open hours.
- Closed-hour snapshots are ignored.
- The dashboard compares the latest open day against previous open days and labels popularity as rising, falling, or stable.
- CEO report export now includes openHoursActivityTrend.

No new environment variables are required.
Keep TSN_STOCK_ALLOWED_USERNAME=ceo.
