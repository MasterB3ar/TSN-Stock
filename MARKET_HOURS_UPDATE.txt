TSN-S v1.1.8 market-hours update

Added scheduled open/closed logic:
- 08:10-09:30 open
- 09:30-09:50 closed
- 09:50-11:15 open
- 11:15-12:00 closed
- 12:00-13:30 open
- 13:30-08:10 closed

The schedule uses Europe/Copenhagen by default via TSN_STOCK_MARKET_TIMEZONE.

While closed:
- Buy/sell requests return HTTP 423 with market status.
- TSNM online-minute rewards are paused.
- Wallet and chart can still load in view-only mode.
- Frontend shows open/closed status and disables buy/sell buttons.

New endpoint:
GET /api/market-hours
